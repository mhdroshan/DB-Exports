Meet The Founder

Mr. Shahood Kurikkal

Founder & CEO

Mr. Shahood Kurikkal carries forward a rich family legacy of agriculture, rooted in the extensive plantations owned by his grandfather. Growing up in this environment, he developed a deep connection with the land and an innate understanding of farming practices.

Leveraging this heritage, he transitioned from traditional farming to processing, venturing into transforming raw plantation bounty into premium final food products. His hands-on experience in farming allows him to expertly discern the quality of produce, ensuring that DB Export services only the finest ingredients.

With a vision to bring India's finest produce to the global stage, he founded DB Export. "Our strength lies in our roots. By understanding the journey from soil to shelf, we ensure our customers receive products that are not just fresh, but cultivated with care and tradition."
