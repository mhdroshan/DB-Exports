Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, delivering Excellence.

Legacy of Quality

DB Export begins its journey from the lush green hills of Kerala, India – the spice capital of the world. With generations of expertise in cultivating and processing spices, we have established ourselves as a premier exporter of high-quality Indian spices.

Our commitment goes beyond business; it is about preserving the authentic taste and aroma that Indian spices are known for globally. We work directly with farmers to ensure that every grain of spice we export meets the highest standards of purity and potency.

Meet The Founder

Mr. Shahood Kurikkal

Founder & CEO

With a deep-rooted passion for agriculture and a vision to bring India's finest produce to the global stage, Mr. Shahood Kurikkal founded DB Export.

Over the years, he has built strong, personal relationships with farmers across South India—from the spice plantations of Kerala to the fruit orchards of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. These direct connections allow DB Export to source premium quality spices, vegetables, and fruits that are often reserved for local markets.

"Our strength lies in our farmers. By working closely with them, we ensure that our customers receive produce that is not just fresh, but also cultivated with care and tradition."
